GENERAL MOVES

FOR PIECES:
[piece]:(starting square)(ending square)
[piece]:(starting square)x(ending square)
For pawns, omit "[piece]:"
If it's not needed you can omit the starting square

CASTLING

Four-tile castles: O{d}O
Five-tile castles: O{d}O{d}O
Replace {d} with the axis of castling
For example, castling along the x-axis is notated OxO, castling along the y-axis is notated OyO, and castling along the v-axis is notated OvO
